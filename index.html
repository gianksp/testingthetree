Thought experiments

Tools for thinking
about origins.

Three interactive experiments exploring inference, continuity, and what we can — and cannot — conclude from biological evidence.

01

Origin of Life Gambling

What are the actual odds? An interactive probability experiment on the spontaneous origin of functional biological sequences.

02

Origami Experiment

Can form tell you history? A hands-on demonstration of the principle of continuity and why similarity alone cannot establish ancestry.

Phylogenetic Tree Visualizer

Explore and interrogate phylogenetic trees built from protein sequence data. Visualize branch support, homoplasy, and the assumptions behind the topology.
